# ASSET OVERVIEW

## Summary

Franklin OnChain U.S. Government Money Fund (FOBXX / Benji) is a regulated tokenized money market fund issued by Franklin Templeton. The fund maintains exposure to U.S. government securities, cash, and repurchase agreements while recording share ownership on blockchain infrastructure.

---

## Key Notes

- SEC-registered money market fund
- Blockchain-recorded share ownership
- Backed primarily by U.S. government securities
- Operated through Benji Investments platform
- One of the earliest institutional tokenized treasury products
- Daily yield accrual structure
