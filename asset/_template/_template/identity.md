## Full Description

USTB is a tokenized money market fund issued by Superstate Trust, a Delaware statutory trust registered with the SEC as an open-end management investment company (CIK 0001982577). The fund invests in short-duration U.S. government securities and issues tokenized shares on Ethereum, Solana, and Plume Mainnet. These tokenized shares represent the same official shares that trade on traditional exchanges—they are not derivatives, wrappers, or separate share classes. Fund assets are custodied offchain at Anchorage Digital Bank N.A., with private key management facilitated by Turnkey. The platform features built-in compliance at the token level, including allowlisted wallets and programmatically enforced transaction restrictions.

The target investors are institutional participants—asset managers, stablecoin issuers, DeFi protocols, and lending protocol liquidators—who require compliant, yield-bearing onchain instruments. Minting and redeeming USTB requires KYC/AML verification, and holding or transferring the token requires prior wallet allowlisting. The Protocol Mint and Redeem upgrade enables composable onchain functions: investors can mint USTB by transferring USDC in a single transaction, and redeem USTB for USDC instantaneously, with initial redemption liquidity of $10M. A custom onchain oracle provides continuous pricing, enabling gas-efficient DeFi composability.

Key differentiators include that USTB represents direct fund shares rather than synthetic exposure, continuous second-by-second pricing, deep DeFi integration (notably Aave Horizon RWA at ~$66.6M TVL and Frax at ~$57.5M TVL), and multi-chain deployment across three networks. The fund's onchain market cap of approximately $770.5M and active DeFi TVL of ~$190.8M position it as one of the larger tokenized treasury products onchain.

Superstate has integrated with Chainlink and Chronicle oracles in addition to its own USTB Oracle for continuous onchain pricing. The fund's DeFi TVL is concentrated across four protocols, with Aave Horizon RWA and Frax/Frax USD each holding roughly 30-35% of the ~$190.8M DeFi active TVL.
