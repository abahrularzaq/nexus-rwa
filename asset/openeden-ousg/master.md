# ASSET OVERVIEW

## Summary

OpenEden OUSG is a tokenized treasury product providing blockchain-native access to U.S. Treasury exposure through regulated institutional-grade underlying assets. The product is designed to deliver stable yield from short-duration government securities while enabling onchain settlement and programmable treasury management.

- Institutional-grade tokenized treasury product
- Exposure to regulated U.S. Treasury instruments
- Yield-bearing NAV structure
