## Full Description

The Superstate Short Duration US Government Securities Fund is a tokenized fund whose ownership is represented by USTB, either as blockchain tokens or through book-entry record keeping. The fund invests in short-duration U.S. Treasury Bills and targets returns in line with the federal funds rate, while seeking current income consistent with liquidity and stability of principal. USTB tokens are issued on Ethereum, Solana, and Plume, and subscriptions and redemptions can be made through USD or USDC with liquidity each market day.

The asset is designed for Qualified Purchasers in supported jurisdictions. Investors must complete onboarding, KYC/AML checks, and wallet allowlisting before holding or transferring USTB onchain. Superstate states that approved investors can add wallets to the allowlist to hold, trade, and use USTB onchain.

USTB differentiates itself through token-level permissioning, allowlisted wallet transfers, multi-network support, and DeFi integrations such as Aave on Ethereum. The fund also supports protocol mint and redeem, allowing investors to mint or redeem shares in a single onchain Ethereum transaction. Its service provider stack includes The Bank of New York Mellon as custodian, Ernst & Young LLP as auditor, NAV Fund Services as NAV calculation agent, and Federated Hermes as sub-advisor.
